FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™ Official Merchandise  E-Commerce Website 

An e-commerce website design, development and social media management project.

Black Arrow Gifts and Novelties, a creative hub of innovation and dedication, embarked on a journey in 2020 with the goal of producing official licensed products for the historic FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™.

Client

Black Arrow Gifts and Novelties

Year

2020

Services

E-commerce Website
Product Design
Social Media

The challenge was monumental: to create an e-commerce platform capable of showcasing a diverse collection of more than 270 different lines of merchandise in various categories including Traditional Home Accessories, Gifting, Fashion Accessories, Jewellery, Games & Cheer Gear.

Our collaborative effort with Black Arrow Gifts and Novelties resulted in the successful launch of their official FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™ merchandise e-commerce website. The website effectively showcased over 270 unique product lines, enabling customers to explore, purchase, and celebrate this historic event.

Our approach, which prioritized user-centric design, customization, security, and performance, led to a successful website that contributed to the commemoration of this historic event. We look forward to continued collaboration with Black Arrow and other clients in shaping innovative digital experiences.
